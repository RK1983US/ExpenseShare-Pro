## ğŸ”§ Common Tasks

### Splitting a Restaurant Bill
1. Add the restaurant expense
2. Enter the total amount
3. Select all people who ate
4. Choose "Equal Split"
5. Save the expense

### Managing Rent & Utilities
1. Create a group for your apartment/house
2. Add all roommates as members
3. Add monthly expenses (rent, electricity, internet, etc.)
4. Use equal split or custom amounts based on room size
5. Check balances to see who owes what

### Trip Expense Tracking
1. Create a group for your trip
2. Add all travelers as members
3. Track expenses as they happen (hotels, meals, transport)
4. Use appropriate split methods (some might not apply to everyone)
5. Generate final report at the end of the trip
